REPPIT Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 14, 2025

Introduction

REPPIT ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information.

REPPIT stores all your data locally on your device using localStorage. This includes:

  • Profile information (name, age, height, weight)
  • Workout logs and exercise history
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, this information will be permanently deleted.

Third-Party Services

REPPIT does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Internet Connection

The app may require an internet connection for initial loading when used as a web app. However, no personal data is transmitted during this process.

Children's Privacy

REPPIT does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general fitness tracking and is suitable for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • View all your data within the app
  • Edit or delete any profile or workout data
  • Delete all data by clearing app storage or uninstalling
